Skin:

Original 2048: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Lucas Sequence: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles touch, they merge into one! To merge two tiles, their sum must be a number of the Lucas Sequence.

Fibonacci Sequence: Use your arrow keys or swipe to move the tiles. When two neigbouring numbers from the Fibonacci sequence touch, they merge into the next one!

Threes: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with sum of 3 or the same number touch greater than 3 or equal to 3, they merge into one!

Merge Any: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When ANY two tiles touch, they merge into one!

Power 2: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!
But every time you make a move, the tiles will spawn in consecutive powers of 2 every single time.

Tile Zero: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!
Watch out for ‘0’s—they can only merge with themselves!

Negative: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same absolute value touch, they merge into one!

Gravity: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!
But every time you make a move, gravity has been enabled, and those tiles above will drop to the bottom of the grid.

Troll: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!
As you make a move, the game will also make another random move for you. Pretty mindless
